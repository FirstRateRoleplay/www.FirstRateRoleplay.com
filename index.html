Welcome to First Rate Roleplay™

Established in 2019 - Not Your Average Roleplay Community!

About Us

First Rate Roleplay™ is an 18+ Roleplaying community that is based out of the Pacific Northwest. With specific influences from Washington, Oregon, and California spiced into the mixture, we create a unique blend of all the best aspects of the PNW to make it feel like home while creating our own lore friendly world.

Our community is a Realistic Life RP community designed with an intent to focus on creating in-depth characters and storylines, rather than the traditional “Cops & Robbers” style of recycled roleplay that is popular in other servers. We prioritize realistic and engaging storytelling with our policies and gameserver mechanics, while ensuring things remain properly balanced as at the end of the day, this is a video game after all and we are all here to have fun in the end.

The overall mission and goal of First Rate Roleplay™ is to be a community that changes the landscape of what people conceive of roleplay gaming altogether. We hope to achieve this goal by providing well developed gameservers, with passionate staff & administration behind the scenes who care about roleplay and for the community as a whole.

It is the ultimate end goal for First Rate Roleplay™ to be known as a community unlike any other in the world. Therefore it is our community’s motto to be “Not Your Average Roleplay Community” and it is our intention to live up to the expectation that our motto insinuates by upholding a better quality of standards than any other community out there.

What We Have To Offer

  • An adult oriented community with an eighteen (18) or older age limit.
  • A community that is based out of the Pacific Northwest.
  • A Content Creator and Streamer friendly environment.
  • Seamless Discord Integration with our Community Assets.

Community Requirements

  • Must be in the community discord server before submitting an application.
  • Must be at least eighteen (18) years or older at the time of applying.
  • Must be able to read, write, and speak fluent English.
  • Must have downloaded and installed the FiveM client.
  • Must have a legally owned copy of GTAV.
  • Must have a microphone of decent quality.
  • Must have a good internet connection.

How to Join

To become a member of our community, just follow these simple steps.

  • Step 1: Read and review our Community Requirements and Terms of Service.
  • Step 2: Join the official community Discord Server using the button on our website.
  • Step 3: When applications are open, fill out the new member application using the button below.
  • Step 4: Once you have filled out the application form, click submit and wait patiently.
  • Step 5: Wait for a staff member to review your application. This can take up to fourteen (14) days.
  • Step 6: Once your application has been reviewed, you will receive an email with more instructions.
    •

    Additional Information About Joining The Community

  • If at any point you leave the official community discord server, it will automatically count as a resignation from the community. If resign from the community, you will have to submit a new application if you wish to rejoin in the future.
  • If your application is accepted, you will be given allowlist access to the server immediately. From there, you will be able to access all of the member's only materials within our Discord and Community Portal and begin your roleplay adventure!
  • However if your application is rejected, you will not be given access to the server and will have to re-apply after a cool down period of a minimum of thirty (30) days from the date of your original application.
